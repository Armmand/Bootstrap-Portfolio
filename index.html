About Me

My name is Armmand Alston and I was born in Durham, North Carolina on April 2, 1988. I lived in Chapel Hill for a short period of time before moving to Southern Pines, North Carolina at the age of five. While in middle school I was highly involved in sports playing soccer, basketball, and track. By the time high school came around I was awarded a full athletic scholarship to The O’Neil high school in Southern Pines North Carolina. During this time I mostly focused on running track. I along with several of my teammates won a couple of state titles during my time at O’Neil. After graduating from high school I started working towards my associates in criminal justice at Sandhills Community College. After completing his degree Armmand began his 8 years career in policing. I currently reside in Durham, North Carolina with a wife and an eleven month old son.

I also love cars and motorcycles.
